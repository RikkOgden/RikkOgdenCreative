Work History

February 1998 - October 2017

I am a self-trained digital creative and website designer. For 19 years I worked for a UK travel company, Chadwell Travel Ltd. Trading as a1travel (initially trading as 'Premier Leisure'), starting out as a holiday salesman. After a few years it was clear that e-commerce was starting to take off, and we needed a web presence. Having an art and design college background, and a passion for design and technology, I propsed that the company gave me the chance to build the company website.

The company kindly gave me a chance to do this, and so I bought a couple of books on HTML and began my journey to becoming a web professional and began to create what would become a successful independent travel provider website, a1travel.com. The rest, they say, is history!

On my quest, I taught myself HTML, Adobe Fireworks, Adobe Flash, Adobe Photoshop, classic ASP and SQL.

I created a database and back-end using classic ASP and MS SQL Server to provide a CMS for holiday deals which were advertised and displayed on the website. We had an integrated e-commerce solution from an industry provider. As technology moved on, the company switched to .NET and outsourced to developers to take care of the back-end, whilst I maintained design, front-end, creative media and project management responsibility

Due to the volatile nature of the industry, on October 20th 2017, the company went into liquidation and I found myself in the unfortunate position of being made redundant.

Man Overboard

October 2017 - December 2017

Finding myself without a job and in open waters I began to do some research and I realised that over the course of the last few years I had stagnated. I found myself in the undesirable position of feeling adrift in a sea of new technology.

To that end I spent the time between October 20th 2017 and the end of December 2017 researching and adapting to the modern workflow and integrating into my own methodologies.
